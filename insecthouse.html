INSECT HOUSE

Some creepy crawlies are not so creepy. Find out more about how these little guys impact the unseen world.

Some Species:

  • Mantis - Mantises are distributed worldwide in temperate and tropical habitats.
  • Brown marmorated stink bug - It is an insect in the family Pentatomidae, native to China, Japan, Korea and other Asian regions.
  • Phasmatodea - They are an order of insects whose members are variously known as stick insects, stick-bugs, walking sticks, stick animals, or bug sticks.
  • Rhino Beetles - Rhinoceros beetles are herbivorous insects named for the horn-like projections on and around the males' heads.
  • Man-faced stink bug - Catacanthus incarnatus, is a fairly common bug that first appeared in New Horizons.
