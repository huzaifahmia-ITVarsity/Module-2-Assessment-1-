LOST FOREST

Welcome to the Lost Forest!

Escape the noise of the city and have a quiet picnic in the Lost Forest, just you and your family. Refresh your body with the clean, fresh air that nature has to offer. Switch of your phones and take in the sights that the park has to offer. Take in the atmosphere and listen to the birds chirping and other ambient sounds resonating throughout the park. Whatever you choose, you can be sure that you and your loved ones will feel at peace and will experience tranquility.
